About Us

Trusted storage in the heart of the Highlands

Highland Motorhome Storage offers secure, private hardstanding storage for your motorhome, campervan, caravan, or boat — just 9 miles from Inverness, serving the communities of Beauly, Dingwall, Muir of Ord and the wider Highland region.

We understand how important your vehicle is. Whether it's a cherished motorhome, a family caravan, or a boat for exploring our beautiful coasts, you want it to be kept safe, secure, and ready for your next adventure.
